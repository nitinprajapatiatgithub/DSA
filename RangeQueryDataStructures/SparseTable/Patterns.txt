================================================================================
SPARSE TABLE PROBLEM PATTERNS
================================================================================

PATTERN 1: STATIC IDEMPOTENT QUERIES (O(1))
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Context: The operation satisfies Policy::is_idempotent = true (e.g., Min, Max, GCD).
* Strategy: Overlap Strategy. 
  Query(L, R) merges two precomputed overlapping intervals of length 2^k.
  k = floor(log2(R - L + 1)).

PATTERN 2: STATIC NON-IDEMPOTENT QUERIES (O(log N))
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Context: The operation is not idempotent (e.g., Sum, XOR).
* Strategy: Bit-Stitching Strategy.
  Query(L, R) decomposes the range length into powers of 2 and merges the 
  disjoint intervals.
